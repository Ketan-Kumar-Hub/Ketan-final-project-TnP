Shipping Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Order Processing & Handling Time

📦 Processing Time: All orders are processed within 2 to 4 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has been shipped.

🚨 Delays: In case of high order volumes or unforeseen postal service issues, slight shipping delays may occur. We appreciate your patience and will update you via email.

2. Shipping Services & Delivery Time

✅ Service Availability: We currently ship only within India.

📮 Shipping Carrier: All orders are shipped via India Post (Speed Post).

🚚 Estimated Delivery Time:

  • Metro Cities: 3 to 5 business days
  • Other Cities: 5 to 7 business days
  • Rural Areas: 7 to 10 business days

3. Shipping Charges

💰 Free Shipping: We provide free shipping on all orders — no additional shipping fees will be applied at checkout.

4. Failed Deliveries & Address Accuracy

📍 Ensure your address is correct at checkout. We are not responsible for delays or non-delivery due to incorrect or incomplete addresses provided by the customer.

5. Lost or Damaged Shipments

🚨 Lost Packages: If your package is lost during transit, please contact us immediately. We will work with the courier to locate your package or arrange a replacement.

💥 Damaged on Delivery: If you receive a damaged product, you must report it within 3 days of delivery with supporting photos or videos.

6. Return to Sender Policy

📦 If a package is returned to us due to incorrect address or repeated failed delivery attempts:

  • Re-shipping charges will apply (paid by the customer).
  • If you choose not to re-ship, a refund (excluding the shipping cost) will be processed.

7. Holiday Shipping Policy

🎄 During holiday seasons, shipping times may be extended due to high demand and courier service limitations. We recommend placing your orders early during peak seasons to avoid delays.
